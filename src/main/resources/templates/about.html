About GardenGlow

It is an application which is based on managing the plants online. This project is all the details about the plants, plants category, plants type, order, sells etc... There is a single user available in the project that is the customer. In this project customer can see plants, plants category etc... customer can buy the plants and see his order details customer can also give feedback about plants.

GardenGlow germinated in 2014 from a promise to make ‘green and healthy’ a click away for all Indians

Having plants in our homes or in our offices doesn’t just look good, it also boosts our mood, makes us more productive, and cleans the air around us by absorbing toxins

Ordering a pizza is easy but ever heard of ordering a plant to your doorstep? This is where GardenGlow comes in.

We believe that Green is Good and are here to enable Indians to access plants in the easiest way possible – online! We are here to shape the future of gardening!

We believe that every space can be made more beautiful with plants! Come, join us in our vision to make all spaces green and healthy!
